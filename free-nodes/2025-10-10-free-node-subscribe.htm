- 精细化路由：可区分国内外流量，仅对目标网站（如TikTok服务器）启用代理

推荐使用在线生成器创建定制化配置：
yaml proxies: - name: "TikTok专用节点" type: vmess server: hk-tiktok01.example.com port: 443 uuid: xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx alterId: 64 cipher: auto tls: true network: ws ws-path: "/video" ws-headers: Host: tiktokcdn.com
